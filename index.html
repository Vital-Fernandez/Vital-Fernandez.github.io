About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in astrophysics at the University of Michigan. I specialize in the analysis of the gas chemical composition and dynamics by studying of photons emitted by ionized atoms in star forming regions.

I am passionate about sharing the algorithms and code I develop for these studies. Currently, I am exploring machine learning models to enhance my software, making it more accessible and useful for the community.

If you would like to learn more, please contact me.
